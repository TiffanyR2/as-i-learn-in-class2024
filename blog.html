Whale Talk


This Is A safe Space For Whale Lovers: Whales are fascinating creatures!

They belong to a widely distributed and diverse group of fully aquatic placental marine mammals. As an informal and colloquial grouping, they correspond to large members of the infraorder Cetacea, which includes all cetaceans apart from dolphins and porpoises. Interestingly, from a formal, cladistic perspective, dolphins and porpoises may also be considered whales



Whales belong to two groups: toothed whales and baleen whales. Toothed whales have teeth and hunt prey, while baleen whales have plates of bristles that filter food from the water.
